ERPNext woocommerce Connector

Sync transactions between woocommerce and ERPNext

ERPNext woocommerce Connector will sync data between your woocommerce and ERPNext accounts.

  1. It will sync Products and Cutomers between woocommerce and ERPNext
  2. It will push Orders from woocommerce to ERPNext
    • If the Order has been paid for in woocommerce, it will create a Sales Invoice in ERPNext and record the corresponding Payment Entry
    • If the Order has been fulfilled in woocommerce, it will create a draft Delivery Note in ERPNext

Install

From your site

To install this app, login to your site and click on "Installer". Search for ERPNext woocommerce and click on "Install"

Using Bench

Go to your bench folder and setup the new app

$ bench get-app erpnext_woocommerce https://github.com/frappe/erpnext_woocommerce
$ bench new-site testsite
$ bench --site testsite install-app erpnext_woocommerce

Login to your site to configure the app.
